About BrightMinds Press

BrightMinds Press is an independent publishing brand specializing in educational books, activity books, study guides, workbooks, journals, and practical nonfiction titles. Our mission is to create engaging, easy-to-understand resources that help children, teenagers, students, professionals, and lifelong learners develop practical knowledge and valuable skills.

What We Publish

  • Children's Educational Books
  • Activity Books
  • Teen Workbooks
  • Vocabulary Books
  • Study Guides
  • Technology Guides
  • Professional Certification Prep Books
  • Health and Fitness Books

Business Operations

BrightMinds Press develops, edits, publishes, and distributes books through Amazon Kindle Direct Publishing (Amazon KDP). Revenue is generated through worldwide royalty payments from book sales across Amazon marketplaces.
